About

Mens Den is a mens peer support group set up in May 2018. We offer a safe place that is non-judgemental to talk about Men's Mental Health and other situations. Men of all backgrounds, age, race and abilities are welcome.

We meet at

Colne Open Door
, Wednesday evening between 6pm and 7:30pm.

The format for our meeting is simple:

  • Check-in - Gives each chap a few minutes to talk about their week; the good, the bad and what is on their mind
  • Chat - Opportunity to grab a coffee and chat about things of interest with others
  • Check-out - Opportunity to sum up how they are feeling and what they plan to do in the week coming

Some good strong friendships are made.
